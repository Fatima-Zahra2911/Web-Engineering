Common Questions

How do online wellness sessions work?

Our online sessions are conducted through our secure platform. You can join live classes or access pre-recorded content at your convenience. All you need is a stable internet connection and a device with a camera for interactive sessions.

What qualifications do your wellness experts have?

All our instructors are certified professionals in their respective fields, including licensed nutritionists, certified fitness trainers, and registered yoga teachers. We regularly verify and update their credentials.

How personalized are the wellness plans?

Each plan is customized based on your initial assessment, goals, and preferences. Our experts consider your current fitness level, dietary requirements, and lifestyle factors to create a comprehensive wellness program.

What are your operating hours?

We are open from 6 AM to 10 PM every day.

Do you offer personal training?

Yes, we have certified personal trainers available.

Can I cancel my membership?

Yes, you can cancel your membership at any time.

Are there group classes?

Yes, we offer a variety of group classes, including yoga, Pilates, and HIIT.
